Ben O'Sullivan

Software Engineer, Composer, Gamer

Hi! I'm Ben.

Ever since I was a young kid, I've always known that I wanted to be in the computer industry. Growing up, I tinkered and experimented with a wide variety of different games and software, until I discovered the concept of programming. At around 12, I began to learn Java so that I could begin modding the infamous Minecraft, and later moved onto Python and then into the world of Web Development. I love learning how to simplify build processes, and figuring out the best way to approach every problem so that I can get it done as quickly as possible.

While I enjoy programming, I am also a big fan of music, and as such compose songs in my spare time. While I only consider it a hobby right now, it allows me to take a break from the world of technology, and leads me to explore my creative side.
